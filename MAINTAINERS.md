# Otoroshi Maintainers

This document describes who maintains Otoroshi and how project stewardship works.

## Current maintainers

Otoroshi is currently maintained by:

- Mathieu Ancelin
- Cloud APIM team

## Maintainer role

Maintainers are responsible for the long-term health of the project. This includes:

- reviewing and merging pull requests,
- discussing and guiding architectural changes,
- ensuring overall consistency of the project,
- maintaining documentation and release quality,
- handling sensitive topics such as security-related changes.

## Decision making

Most decisions are made through discussion and pragmatic consensus.

For larger changes affecting architecture, security, or long-term direction, core maintainers have final decision authority.

## Becoming a maintainer

Maintainers are invited based on sustained, high-quality contributions and demonstrated understanding of the project.

There is no automatic process. Maintainership is based on trust, consistency, and long-term involvement.

## Inactive maintainers

Maintainers who are inactive for an extended period may be removed from the active maintainers list in order to keep this document accurate.

## Amendments

This document may evolve over time through pull requests and maintainer discussion.
